# Abnormal Labor

## Etiology

- Abnormal contractions.
- Abnormal birth canal.
- Abnormal fetus.

## Clinical Presentation

- **Mother**:
  - Exhaustion.
  - Weak or excessive uterine contractions.
  - Cervical edema or slow progress.
  - Delayed or stalled descent of the presenting part.
